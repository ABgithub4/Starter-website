Facts about Horses

Here are some interesting facts about Horses:

  • Obligate nasal breathers - horses are unable to breathe through their mouthes.
  • You can estimate a Horses age by inspecting their teeth.
  • Horses are able to sleep standing up, although they do need to lie down to achieve a full cycle of sleep.

The most common breeds in the UK are:

  1. Thouroughbreds - Popularly trained for horse racing, this breed is characterised for their speed, strength and athleticism.
  2. Arabian horse - Regarded as one of the oldest breeds in history, these animals originated in the Arabian peninsula.

  3. Mustang - Free roaming wild horse originating in North america. Known traits are their speed, adaptibility, long distances.
